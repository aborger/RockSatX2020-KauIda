Power Lamp
The lamp indicates the camera status when the power is on.

Lit in blue:
There is a sufficient battery level.

Flashing in blue:
The battery level is low.

Flashing in red:
The power is turning off.

Lit in green:
Starting to charge.

Flashing in green:
Low Battery.

Unlit:
Charging is complete, the camera is sleeping, or the power is off.
